I am Peruvian born Amsterdam based Graphic Designer working accross the fields of Visual culture, Fashion and Performance. Over the past years I have developed concepts, visual identites and artworks for cultural institutions, brands and artists. I am an image-maker with a focus on typography and a good eye for details. I do enjoy working with a team and since 2017 I run avoidstreet. A fashion label that uses discarded apparel to create ‘streetcouture’.
