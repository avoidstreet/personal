I am a Peruvian born Amsterdam based Graphic Designer working across the fields of Visual culture, Performance and Fashion. I am interested in hidden structures and systems of display that organize our daily life. Energy, Collaboration & Sustainability are the main focuses of my projects.

Since 2017 I run @avoidstreet a fashion label that uses discarded apparel to create ‘street couture’: customised ready-to-wear for site specific interventions. Building a world around clothes, Fashion becomes a tool to generate content — providing Clothes, Jpegs & Lyrics for a cultural and tailor-made audience.
